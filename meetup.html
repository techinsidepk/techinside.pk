Meetup For Techinside.pk

overview

Entrepreneurs or coders who want to learn about alternative technologies, and who want to learn and share the non technical aspects of startup life.

Pakistan may not be best known for its tech scene, but it has a thriving community of techies and on-the-edge actors that deserve to be known world-wide. This group intend to create such an environment so a rich and fruitful interaction can take place.

3 things define this meetup:

  • It's informal (no nametags, no structure to the event)
  • Monthly
  • It's friendly (just come and say "Hi" to anyone there)
  • Remotely, Yay! ... we will make sure each event we have live speaker

So, if you're a part of the startup and tech community, this is your place to come and hang out and enjoy the wealth of cultural diversity.
